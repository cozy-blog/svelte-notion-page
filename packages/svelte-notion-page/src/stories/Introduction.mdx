# Svelte Notion Page!!

This is library that helps you to render notion page in svelte! Of course you can customize any component into what you want to be.

<br />
<br />

## Usage

<Source
  language='jsx'
  dark
  format={false}
  code={dedent`
  <Notion>
    <Notion.Cover src={src} alt={alt} />
    <Notion.Body>
      <Notion.Title title={title} />
      <Notion.Blocks blocks={blocks} />
    </Notion.Body>
  </Notion>
`}
/>
